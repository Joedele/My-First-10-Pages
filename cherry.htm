 CHERRY

Cherries are small, round fruits that burst with flavor, ranging from sweet to tart. Rich in antioxidants, vitamins A and C, and melatonin, they promote good sleep, reduce inflammation, and support overall health.

Cherries are often enjoyed fresh, in jams, or baked into desserts like pies. Their deep red color and juicy taste make them an irresistible snack or ingredient in sweet and savory dishes.
