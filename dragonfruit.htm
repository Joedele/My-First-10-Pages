 DRAGON FRUIT

Dragon fruit, or pitaya, is an exotic tropical fruit with a striking pink or yellow skin and white or red pulp dotted with tiny black seeds. Known for its mild, sweet taste and refreshing texture, it is a great source of vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants.

This nutrient-dense fruit is not only delicious but also visually appealing, making it popular in smoothie bowls and fruit salads. Its low-calorie content and hydrating properties make it a favorite among health enthusiasts.
