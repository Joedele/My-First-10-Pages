 GRAPE

Grapes are small, sweet fruits that come in green, red, or purple varieties. Packed with vitamins C and K, as well as powerful antioxidants like resveratrol, grapes support heart health and boost energy.

They are commonly eaten fresh, dried into raisins, or used to make wine and juices. Their natural sweetness and versatility make grapes a beloved fruit worldwide.
