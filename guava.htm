GUAVA

Guava is a tropical fruit with a unique sweet and tangy flavor, often enjoyed with its edible seeds and skin. It is rich in vitamin C, dietary fiber, and antioxidants, promoting digestion, skin health, and immunity.

Guavas can be eaten fresh, juiced, or made into jams and jellies. Their versatility and health benefits make them a popular choice in tropical regions and beyond.
