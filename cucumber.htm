 CUCUMBER

Cucumbers are refreshing, hydrating fruits often mistaken for vegetables. They are composed of about 95% water, making them an excellent choice for staying cool and hydrated. Rich in vitamins K and C, cucumbers also support skin health and digestion.

They are a versatile ingredient, ideal for salads, sandwiches, and infused water. Their mild taste and crunchy texture make cucumbers a favorite in healthy snacks and beauty routines.
