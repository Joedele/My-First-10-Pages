 APPLES

Apples are one of the most widely consumed fruits in the world, known for their sweet, crisp texture and versatility. Packed with vitamins like C and antioxidants, they make a nutritious snack that promotes heart health and boosts the immune system. Whether eaten raw, baked into pies, or pressed into cider, apples come in countless varieties such as Fuji, Granny Smith, and Red Delicious.

The fruit is a symbol of health and is famously associated with the saying, "An apple a day keeps the doctor away." Besides their health benefits, apples are easy to store and carry, making them a convenient option for meals and snacks anytime.
